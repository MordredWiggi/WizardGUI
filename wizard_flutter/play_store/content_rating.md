# Content rating + target audience + ads — Play Console answers

These three forms (under **App content**) are quick once you know the answers.

## Content rating (IARC questionnaire)

**Category to choose at the start:** *Reference, News, or Educational*
is technically wrong — Wizard ships in the **Card** game subcategory, so
pick **Game** when prompted. The questionnaire then asks about gameplay
content.

| Question (paraphrased) | Answer |
|---|---|
| Does your app contain violence? | **No** |
| Does your app contain sexual content or nudity? | **No** |
| Does your app contain crude humor or mature themes? | **No** |
| Does your app contain profanity or crude language? | **No** |
| Does your app reference drugs, alcohol, or tobacco? | **No** |
| Does your app contain real or simulated gambling? | **No** — Wizard is a strategy/skill card game with prediction-based scoring; no wagering, currency, or chance-based reward |
| Does your app collect or share user location? | **No** |
| Does your app contain user-generated content visible to other users? | **Yes** (limited) — players type their own display names, which appear on the group's leaderboard. There is no chat, free-text content, comments, or images. Player names are short and only visible to other members of the same group (or globally on the public leaderboard if uploaded there). |
| Does your app allow users to interact with each other? | **No** — there is no in-app messaging or social interaction beyond seeing names + scores |
| Does your app share personal info with other users? | **Yes** — only the display name typed for that game, alongside the score, and only with members of the chosen leaderboard |
| Does your app enable purchases? | **No** |

**Expected rating:** PEGI 3 / ESRB Everyone / IARC 3+. The user-generated-name
disclosure may push this to PEGI 7 in some jurisdictions; that's fine.
